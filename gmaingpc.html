Best Gaming PC's of 2024

Top picks for gaming enthusiasts

Alienware Aurora R15

The Alienware Aurora R15 is a high-performance gaming PC featuring the latest Intel Core i9 or AMD Ryzen 7000 series processors, paired with powerful GPUs like the NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090 or AMD Radeon RX 7900 XTX. It includes advanced liquid cooling and customizable RGB lighting. With support for up to 64GB of DDR5 RAM and 4TB SSD storage, it’s ideal for demanding games and streaming. Prices start around ₹3,00,000 (~$3,600).

HP Omen 45L

The HP Omen 45L offers high-end performance with Intel or AMD processors and NVIDIA or AMD GPUs. It features a unique "Cryo Chamber" cooling system for efficient temperature control and customizable RGB lighting. The system is easy to upgrade, making it a great long-term investment. Prices start at ₹2,50,000 (~$3,000).

MSI Trident X

The MSI Trident X is a compact gaming PC with Intel or AMD processors and top-tier NVIDIA GeForce RTX GPUs. Despite its small size, it offers excellent gaming performance with a sleek design and customizable RGB lighting. Prices start at ₹2,00,000 (~$2,400).

Lenovo Legion Tower 7i

The Lenovo Legion Tower 7i features Intel Core i9 or i7 processors and NVIDIA RTX 40-series GPUs. It offers strong performance with efficient cooling and a clean design. With options for up to 32GB of RAM, it’s a solid and affordable gaming PC starting at ₹1,50,000 (~$1,800).
